SafeHaven

SafeHaven's mission is to bridge the gap of communication between people who desire to serve their community, and those who need to evacuate their homes due to natural disasters like floods.

SafeHaven helps you find a shelter with the help of people that opt to volunteer their homes to help others in need and by recommending government hosted shelter locations.

SafeHaven is a community driven sense of togetherness. We create a sense of trust and safety by helping alleviate anxiety and create a sense of calm for those in need.
